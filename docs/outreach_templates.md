# Design-Partner Outreach Email Templates

Use these templates for cold outreach to design-partner candidates from each buyer persona. Send one at a time — do not CC across personas. Personalize the bracketed fields before sending.

All templates assume the recipient has not heard of the Livability Risk Engine. Do not attach decks on the first email. The goal is a 20-minute call, not a sale.

---

## Outreach notes

- **Do not** send more than two follow-ups per contact.
- **Do not** share pricing in the first email — the goal is a call, not a transaction.
- **Do not** promise features outside the Chicago MVP (multi-city, user accounts, real-time feeds).
- **Do** personalize the firm name, a recent deal or initiative, and the specific pain point most relevant to their role.
- **Do** offer to send a sample response for an address they know — it converts curiosity into a real conversation faster than any pitch.
- Track outreach in a simple spreadsheet: contact name, firm, persona, date sent, reply status, and next action.
